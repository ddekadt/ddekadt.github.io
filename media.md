## short form writing

I have written for numerous news and opinion outlets including the Business Day, Mail & Guardian, Washington Post, and others. I have also appeared on both the BBC and eNCA news. If you are looking for written or interview commentary on South African politics, please reach out.

Below are some examples of of my short-form writing:

South Africa 2024: Everything has changed, but nothing is different. [Good Authority](https://goodauthority.org/news/south-africa-2024-election-anc-ramaphosa-zuma-unity-government/).

South Africa's Healthy Democracy: Why Zuma's Resignation is a Good Sign. 02-20-2018. [Foreign Affairs](https://www.foreignaffairs.com/articles/south-africa/2018-02-20/south-africas-healthy-democracy). (with others)

Here Are 4 Reasons That South Africa's ANC Lost Ground in This Month's Election. 08-12-2016. [WaPo Monkey Cage Blog](https://www.washingtonpost.com/news/monkey-cage/wp/2016/08/12/here-are-4-reasons-that-south-africas-african-national-congress-lost-ground-in-this-months-election/). (with others)
